Reverend Emmanuel and Lady Pastor Denise Anatsui founded Sowseeds International Ministries (SIMs) in 2010 with a vision and heart to see people walk in the fullness of who they are in Christ and to fulfill their God-given destiny. Their vision continues to become a reality as they see people connecting with Christ, their local church and the call of God upon their lives.

SIMs is a diverse church with some 60 nationalities represented in the congregation. The church has been unified by their singular focusâ€”building great people. Through weekly services, family conferences and leadership events Reverend Emmanuel and Lady Pastor Denise Anatsui continue to minister the truth of the Word of God in a practical, real way that reaches all ages.

Reverend Emmanuel is the Senior Pastor and equips and empowers ministry leaders to be courageous and simply take the next step in their God given assignment. Lady Pastor Denise currently serves as the Executive Director of Ladies Of Vision Virtue and Excellence (LOVVE) for the past 10 years.
